Main Projects

Internet of Things: Smart Plant Pot

A smart plant pot for growing small house plants which will include features such as:

  • Voice Commands for watering the plant and checking the plant's status
  • Moisture sensor for detecting the water levels in the soil
  • Light sensor for measuring the light sensitivity for the plant
  • and more features to be added...

Senior Design Project: Improving Unreal Engine's AI

This project aims to improve Unreal Engine's path tracing AI to achieve:

  • A more realistic player-to-Computer engagement
  • An immersive sense of competition

This project also aims to include:

  • A more realistic lighting and physics system
  • Physics and AI interaction
